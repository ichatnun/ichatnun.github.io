About Me

I am a researcher at the National Nanotechnology Center (NANOTEC), National Science and Technology Development Agency (NSTDA), Thailand , where I have led the Nanoinformatics and Artificial Intelligence (NAI) Research Team since 2021. My research interests include machine learning, signal processing, and mathematical optimization.
